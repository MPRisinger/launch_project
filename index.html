Welcome

This podcast was created to fill a void that CTRL X left. Still being about art and culture, this podcast delivers lessons, anecdotes, and helpful discussions about sports media. Each episode is hosted by Matthew Risinger and features a special guest, someone who is an expert in their respective field. No matter where you are, listening to The Risinger Experience will bring you knowledge and pleasure.

Special guests include: Tyson Beck, Chris Ard, Kenton Hessler, Daniel Goldfarb, Samuel Silverman, Christopher Stoney, Meech Robinson, Tony Hunyh, and many more!
